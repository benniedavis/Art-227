About Us

WE are glad that you have visit our website and here to experience our potentials. This organization was founded by Mr. Bennie Davis during his bachelor degree program at the Jiandgxi University of Engineering located in the Jiangxi Province in china. It is our hope that as you have come, you can join us in making this Non-Profit Organization a great one.

The Revolutionary Notion is a non-profit organization with the aim and objective of helping young liberians by creating a digital platform in which young folks can learn basic computer skills including:
